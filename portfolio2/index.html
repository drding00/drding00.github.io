Hi There

You know, there’s something really fantastic about the night sky. I mean besides it being lovely to look at, it has this sort of limitlessness about it. Just this wide open expanse where anything could exist. I like to think about web development the same way.

My name’s Danielle and I’ve been putting code between "<'s" and ">'s" for a little over a decade now. Yes, I remember a time building entire page layouts with tables *shutter*, then frames, then divs and the new structural elements via HTML5. My point is, my journey has been a continuous one. Striving to strengthen my skills, my work process, and ultimately, my end product.

Will I ever acquire all there is to know about HTML, CSS, or JavaScript? Nope. And I’m excited about that. Because it means I'll always be learning and improving. Each project is a new adventure. Each time I crack open a text editor; it's another chance to make something cool.

And that is awesome.
